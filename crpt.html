Corporate Wars

Welcome

Welcome to the Corporate Wars! In this competition, you and your team will navigate through four rounds, each designed to test your strategic skills and business acumen.

Role of Participant

As a participant, you'll have to:

  • BUY WISELY IN AUCTION:

    In the auction round, act wisely and purchase the commodity or theme of the event within the given basic amount, considering other rounds.

  • SEEK INVESTMENT WISELY IN THE PITCHING ROUND:

    Showcase your entrepreneurship skills and request necessary investments from the jury or investors.

  • DISTRIBUTE AND PRESENT WISELY:

    Handle this round with care, as the outcome will impact your results. Present your distribution and plan thoughtfully.

Prerequisites

  • Form a team of 3-4 members.
  • Have a keen interest in entrepreneurship and problem-solving.
  • Have a brief overview of how things work in a professional business pitch or be wise enough to scale up your local bargaining skills into a professional pitch so that the investors lay a hand on you.

Guidelines

  • Integrity: Always act honestly and uphold ethical standards.
  • Professionalism: Dress appropriately, communicate respectfully, and exhibit a positive attitude. Treat all participants, mentors, and judges with respect.
  • Collaboration: Contribute actively to your team, listen to others, share knowledge, and help peers when possible. Foster a cooperative and inclusive environment.
  • Responsibility: Be accountable for your actions and commitments.
  • Safety: Prioritize safety in all activities, adhere to all the safety protocols.
