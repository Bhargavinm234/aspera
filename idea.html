Idea to Enterprise

Welcome

Welcome to Idea to Enterprise! This exciting overnight competition is your chance to transform innovative ideas into real businesses. Join us on June 1st from 4 PM onwards for an immersive experience where creativity meets entrepreneurship. Role of Participant

As a participant, you'll work in a team of 3-4 members to tackle a challenging problem statement. Your journey will involve brainstorming innovative solutions, creating prototypes, and developing a business plan. You'll engage in activities that cover everything from initial concept development to final presentations. Along the way, you'll also devise marketing strategies to attract potential customers and make your business stand out. By the end of the event, you'll have a well-rounded business portfolio ready for potential investors.

Prerequisites

To participate in Idea to Enterprise, you should:

  • Be a current student (undergraduate or postgraduate) from any discipline.
  • Form a team of 3-4 members.
  • Have a keen interest in entrepreneurship and problem-solving.
  • Be ready for an intensive, overnight event filled with creativity and teamwork.
  • Pay a registration fee of 200 INR per team.

Guidelines

  • Team Composition: Teams must consist of 3-4 members.
  • Registration: Register your team with a fee of 200 INR.
  • Problem Statements: You will select a problem statement from a provided list at the start of the event.
  • Conduct: Maintain a professional and collaborative attitude throughout the competition.
  • Originality: All work must be original and created during the event. Plagiarism or pre-developed solutions will lead to disqualification.
  • Submission: Follow the submission guidelines provided during the event for each phase of the competition.
  • Judgment: The decisions of the judges are final and binding.
