The Legal Showdown

Welcome

Join us for The Legal Showdown at E-Week 2024, a mock trial event that challenges your legal and strategic skills. Play the roles of both lawyers and witnesses in a high-stakes courtroom drama, and compete for exciting cash prizes!

Role of Participant

As a participant, you will:
- Lawyer: Deliver opening and closing statements, conduct examinations and cross-examinations.
- Witness: Provide testimonies and support your team's case with believable performances.
- Also a strategist: Collaborate with your team to develop a compelling case strategy.

Prerequisites

There are no prerequisites. All skill levels are welcome to participate.

Guidelines

- Preparation: Understand case materials and roles.
- Conduct: Maintain professionalism and respect courtroom rules.
- Evidence: Use relevant and permissible evidence.
- Collaboration: Work effectively with your team.
- Adaptability: Respond to opposition and unexpected developments.
