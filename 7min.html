The 7th Minute

Welcome

"7th Minute" is a team activity involving two buying rounds of 7 minutes each, followed by a selling round. Creativity is vital as teams compete to maximize their profit during the selling round.

Role of Participant

Participants engage in strategic negotiation and purchasing during the buying rounds. They analyze market conditions and employ innovative approaches to secure high prices for their purchased items during the selling round. Effective teamwork, creativity, and adaptability are essential for success in this dynamic competition.

Prerequisites

To participate in the "7th Minute" game, individuals should possess a basic understanding of negotiation strategies, market dynamics, and pricing mechanisms. Teams must consist of 3-5 members with diverse skill sets, including analytical thinking, communication, and decision-making abilities.

Guidelines

Teams must adhere to the provided guidelines throughout the competition, maintaining professionalism, integrity, and respect for fellow participants. All decisions and actions should be original, avoiding plagiarism or pre-developed solutions. Submission deadlines and judging criteria will be communicated clearly, and the decisions of the judges are final and binding.
