Welcome

Welcome to The Legal Showdown, where participants engage in simulated courtroom debates, representing plaintiffs and defendants. They present arguments, witnesses, and evidence, honing legal advocacy and critical thinking skills in a dynamic, hands-on environment.

Role of Participant

Participants will take on the roles of plaintiffs, defendants, and lawyers. They will prepare cases, gather evidence, and present their arguments before a judge and jury. This role-playing exercise aims to simulate real courtroom experiences and provide practical insights into the legal process.

Prerequisites

A basic understanding of legal principles and courtroom procedures is beneficial. Participants should have a keen interest in law and advocacy. Prior experience in debate or public speaking is advantageous but not mandatory.

Guidelines

Participants should adhere to the provided guidelines, maintain decorum, and respect the roles assigned. They are encouraged to collaborate with teammates, research thoroughly, and present their cases convincingly. Ethical conduct and professional behavior are paramount.
